## Reference Management Made for LaTeX

CiteDrive helps you collect, organize, and cite sources in LaTeX, Overleaf, Markdown, and more. Designed specifically for academic writing, it supports both BibTeX and BibLaTeX — no plugins, no manual syncing, just clean `.bib` files always up to date.

Use it solo or with your team. CiteDrive fits seamlessly into your workflow whether you're writing a thesis, journal paper, or collaborative project.
